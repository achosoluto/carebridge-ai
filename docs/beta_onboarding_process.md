# CareBridge AI - Beta User Onboarding Process
**Version**: 1.0
**Date**: November 29, 2025
**Process Owner**: Beta Program Team

---

## Overview

This document outlines the complete onboarding process for beta clinic participants. The process is designed to be completed within 2-4 hours and will ensure clinics are fully ready to use CareBridge AI effectively.

**Onboarding Goal**: Get clinic staff comfortable with basic features within 1 day of access.

---

## Pre-Onboarding (24 hours before access)

### Automated Steps
1. **System Preparation**
   - Create clinic account in system
   - Set up default permissions and access levels
   - Configure clinic-specific settings (language, timezone)
   - Prepare welcome email template with credentials

2. **Resource Preparation**
   - Prepare user guides for the clinic's specific specialty
   - Gather clinic contact information
   - Schedule welcome call (if requested)
   - Prepare training materials and videos

### Manual Steps
1. **Review Clinic Information**
   - Verify clinic specialty and common patient languages
   - Identify primary contact person
   - Review any special requirements or preferences
   - Prepare customized welcome package

---

## Day 0 - Welcome & Access Setup (2 hours)

### Hour 1: Initial Contact & Credential Setup
- **9:00 AM**: Send welcome email with:
  - Login URL
  - Temporary credentials
  - Quick-start guide link
  - Calendar invite for welcome call (optional)

- **9:30 AM**: Welcome call (15 minutes)
  - Introduce the product and key features
  - Explain the beta program goals and expectations
  - Provide timeline overview
  - Offer to answer initial questions

- **10:00 AM**: Account activation
  - Clinic receives credentials
  - First login to change temporary password
  - Basic profile setup
  - System tour (guided or self-directed)

### Hour 2: Initial Configuration
- **10:15 AM**: Clinic information setup
  - Enter clinic name and contact info
  - Configure preferred languages
  - Set up staff accounts (up to 5 users)
  - Connect clinic phone number for SMS

- **10:45 AM**: Basic navigation training
  - Dashboard overview
  - Patient message section
  - Appointment management
  - Settings and preferences

---

## Day 1 - Feature Training (2 hours)

### Morning Session: Core Features (1 hour)
- **9:00 AM**: Translation functionality training
  - How messages are translated
  - Confidence indicators
  - When to escalate to human
  - Quality feedback mechanisms

- **9:30 AM**: SMS communication workflow
  - Receiving patient messages
  - Viewing translations
  - Responding to patients
  - Confirming appointments via SMS

### Afternoon Session: Advanced Features (1 hour)
- **2:00 PM**: Appointment management
  - Creating appointments
  - Sending confirmations
  - Managing patient schedules
  - Integration with SMS notifications

- **2:30 PM**: Reporting and feedback
  - Accessing usage reports
  - Providing feature feedback
  - Reporting translation issues
  - Getting help and support

---

## Day 2 - Practice Session (1-2 hours)

### Guided Practice
- **9:00 AM**: Practice scenario 1
  - Receive simulated patient message in Korean
  - Review translation accuracy
  - Respond appropriately
  - Confirm understanding with patient

- **9:30 AM**: Practice scenario 2
  - Schedule appointment via system
  - Send confirmation SMS
  - Handle modification request
  - Document any issues

### Self-Directed Practice
- **10:00 AM**: Clinic staff explores features independently
- **10:30 AM**: Q&A session with support team
- **11:00 AM**: Feedback collection on initial experience

---

## Day 3 - Go-Live Preparation

### Final Preparations
- **9:00 AM**: Review of common questions
- **9:30 AM**: Troubleshooting guide review
- **10:00 AM**: Support contact confirmation
- **10:15 AM**: Feedback channel setup

### Soft Launch
- **10:30 AM**: Begin processing real patient messages
- **Monitor closely for first 2 hours**
- **Be available for immediate support**

## Common Onboarding Issues & Solutions

### Issue: Difficulty with translation quality
**Solution**: 
- Emphasize that 95%+ accuracy is validated
- Show how to identify and flag problematic translations
- Provide examples of high-accuracy phrases
- Schedule follow-up to review specific concerns

### Issue: SMS integration problems
**Solution**:
- Verify clinic phone number format
- Check carrier-specific settings
- Test with multiple phone numbers
- Provide alternative communication methods if needed

### Issue: Staff resistance to new system
**Solution**:
- Emphasize time-saving benefits with concrete examples
- Provide one-on-one training for resistant staff
- Show clear ROI in terms of communication efficiency
- Connect with other clinic staff for peer encouragement

## Escalation Process

### Level 1: System Support
- **Issues**: Login problems, basic feature questions
- **Contact**: beta-support@carebridge.ai
- **Response time**: Within 2 hours during business hours

### Level 2: Technical Issues
- **Issues**: Translation failures, SMS delivery problems
- **Contact**: tech-support@carebridge.ai
- **Response time**: Within 1 hour during business hours

### Level 3: Critical Issues
- **Issues**: System down, data integrity, security concerns
- **Contact**: Direct escalation to on-call engineer
- **Response time**: Within 15 minutes 24/7
