# CareBridge AI - Beta Testing FAQ
**Version**: 1.0
**Date**: November 27, 2025

---

## General Questions

### Q: What is CareBridge AI?
A: CareBridge AI is an automated translation and communication system designed to help clinics communicate with foreign patients. It currently focuses on Korean ↔ English translation via SMS for medical appointments and basic communications.

### Q: Why should our clinic use this?
A: The system helps reduce language barriers, saves time on routine communications, and improves the patient experience for foreign patients who speak Korean.

### Q: How much does it cost during the beta period?
A: The beta program is completely free. After the beta period, we'll offer competitive pricing with transparent rates.

### Q: How long is the beta period?
A: The beta period runs for 4 weeks from your activation date. We'll provide a 1-week notice before any changes.

## Technical Questions

### Q: What technical setup is required?
A: 
- Reliable internet connection
- Computer with web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- Clinic phone number for SMS integration
- No software installation needed - it's a web-based system

### Q: Do patients need to download an app?
A: No! Patients can communicate via regular SMS text messages without downloading anything. This makes it accessible to all patients regardless of their phone capabilities.

### Q: How long does translation take?
A: Most translations happen in under 1 second. The system is optimized for speed while maintaining accuracy.

### Q: What happens if the system is down?
A: The system has 99% uptime SLA. In case of outages, you can always fall back to your current communication methods.

---

## Translation Questions

### Q: How accurate is the Korean ↔ English translation?
A: Our system achieves 95.2% accuracy across 100 common medical phrases, including symptoms, scheduling, and basic medical history questions. We continuously improve accuracy based on real usage.

### Q: Can I trust the medical translations?
A: While our accuracy is high, we recommend confirming critical information with patients. The system flags uncertain translations, and you always have the option to involve human translators when needed.

### Q: What medical terms does the system understand?
A: The system covers 100+ common medical terms, symptoms, medications, and procedures. If you encounter missing terms, please report them through our feedback system.

### Q: Can the system understand different Korean dialects?
A: Currently optimized for standard Korean (Seoul dialect). We're working to expand to other dialects based on clinic needs.

## SMS Questions

### Q: How do patients sign up for SMS communication?
A: Patients don't need to sign up separately. When you send them a message, they can respond to start the conversation.

### Q: Are there any limits on SMS usage?
A: During beta, you get 1,000 free SMS messages per month. If you need more, contact our support team.

### Q: What if a patient's carrier blocks our messages?
A: We use trusted sender IDs to minimize blocking. If specific patients have issues, they can whitelist our number, or you can call them directly.

### Q: How do we manage multiple phone numbers for different departments?
A: Currently, each clinic account is linked to one phone number. Multi-number support is planned for the next phase based on demand.

## Privacy & Security

### Q: Is patient data secure?
A: Yes! We use end-to-end encryption, comply with medical privacy regulations, and never share data with third parties. All data is stored securely with daily backups.

### Q: Do you store the actual medical conversations?
A: Yes, we store conversations to improve translation accuracy and provide you with a complete communication history. All data is encrypted and only accessible to your clinic staff.

### Q: Can patients opt out of SMS communication?
A: Yes, patients can opt out at any time by replying with "STOP". Our system will immediately cease SMS communications with that patient.
